About the Firm

A full-service Company Secretarial, Corporate Governance, and Regulatory Advisory Firm

We are a full-service Company Secretarial, Corporate Governance, and Regulatory Advisory Firm with over 30 years of combined professional experience in advising corporates, investment structures, regulated entities, and growth-stage businesses.

Our practice is built on a strong foundation of compliance integrity, governance discipline, and regulatory foresight. We advise entities operating within India and across international jurisdictions, supporting them through complex statutory, regulatory, and transaction-driven compliance requirements.

The firm regularly works with Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs), private and public companies, NBFCs, manufacturing entities, startups, and investment-backed structures. We act as long-term compliance and governance partners, enabling clients to meet mandatory and non-negotiable statutory obligations while aligning compliance with commercial objectives.

Our Practice Philosophy

We believe that corporate compliance is not a checklist exercise, but a critical governance function. Our advisory approach is:

Preventive

Rather than reactive — anticipating regulatory requirements before they become issues.

Practical

Rather than theoretical — delivering actionable solutions that work in real business contexts.

Transaction-Oriented

Rather than template-driven — customizing our approach to each client's specific needs.

Technical Expertise with Hands-On Execution

We combine technical expertise with hands-on execution, ensuring accuracy, timeliness, confidentiality, and regulatory defensibility in every engagement. Our team stays current with evolving regulatory landscapes while maintaining the practical wisdom gained from decades of professional practice.
