Practice Areas

Comprehensive advisory services across corporate law, governance, and regulatory compliance

01

Company Law & Secretarial Practice

  • Incorporation and structuring of Companies, LLPs, and foreign entities (LO/BO/PO)
  • End-to-end ROC compliances, statutory disclosures, and maintenance of records and registers
  • Alteration of MOA & AOA, capital restructuring, rights issues, private placements, ESOPs, buybacks, and other corporate actions
  • Appointment, resignation, and remuneration matters of Directors, Auditors, and Key Managerial Personnel (KMP)
  • Convening and conducting of Board Meetings, General Meetings, and Committees, including drafting of notices, agendas, and minutes
  • Voluntary liquidation, strike-off, revival, and closure of entities
02

Corporate Governance & Board Advisory

  • Governance framework design and implementation
  • Board effectiveness and compliance advisory
  • Drafting and review of Board Charters, Codes of Conduct, Governance Policies, and Internal Controls
  • Secretarial audits, governance audits, and compliance risk assessments
03

FEMA, RBI & Cross-Border Regulatory Advisory

  • Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) structuring and reporting
  • RBI Filings including FC-GPR, FC-TRS, downstream investments, and FLA returns
  • External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) advisory and ongoing compliance
  • Compounding of contraventions under FEMA
  • Cross-border transaction support and regulatory coordination
04

NBFC & Financial Services Compliance

  • Advisory and ongoing compliance support for NBFCs and regulated Financial entities
  • RBI regulatory filings, disclosures, and governance requirements
  • Assistance with restructuring, investments, and regulatory reporting
  • Compliance oversight aligned with prudential and governance norms
05

CSR, ESG & Sustainability Advisory

  • CSR applicability analysis and compliance under the Companies Act, 2013
  • Preparation of CSR policies, project reports, budgets, and impact documentation
  • Ongoing CSR reporting and statutory disclosures
  • Advisory support for NGOs and implementing agencies
  • ESG-linked governance and disclosure advisory
06

Labour Laws & Employment Compliance

  • Advisory on key labour and employment laws applicable to corporates
  • Structuring and periodic compliance support for workforce-related regulations
  • Drafting and vetting of employment policies, POSH policies, and employee handbooks
  • Compliance alignment for manufacturing and service sector entities
07

Transaction Support, Due Diligence & Documentation

  • Legal and secretarial due diligence for investments, acquisitions, and restructurings
  • Transaction documentation support including SPAs, BTAs, shareholders' agreements, and ancillary documents
  • Pre- and post-transaction regulatory compliances under applicable laws
08

Indirect Tax & Regulatory Coordination

  • Coordination and compliance support for GST and other indirect tax matters
  • Integration of tax compliances with corporate and regulatory obligations
09

Startup, Investment & Growth Advisory

  • Angel investments and early-stage funding advisory
  • Venture capital transaction support
  • Corporate and investment structuring
  • Data privacy and fintech regulatory frameworks
  • End-to-end compliance management enabling founders to focus on business growth
